About Me

I'm open to new roles and collaborations. Feel free to reach out if my background aligns with your team's goals.

With a boundless passion for interactive technology and new media, I find great joy in delving into cutting-edge technologies. My research spans Human-Computer Interaction, Virtual Reality Learning Environments, multimodal perception, accessible design, and AI policy. I am currently completing my MSc thesis — Re-Designing VRLE for Effective Pedagogy — in collaboration with EPFL.

My background in problem solving and interest in problem finding benefits me across the Double Diamond design process. If you're interested in reaching out, hiring me for consulting work, or collaborating on your next endeavour, don't hesitate to contact me.
