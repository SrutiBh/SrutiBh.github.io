About Me

I'm open to new roles and collaborations. Feel free to reach out if my background aligns with your team's goals.

With a boundless passion for interactive technology and new media, I find great joy in delving into cutting-edge technologies. My research spans Human-Computer Interaction, Virtual Reality Learning Environments, multimodal perception, accessible design, and AI policy.

My background in problem solving and interest in problem finding benefits me across the Double Diamond design process. I'm currently completing my MSc thesis — Re-Designing VRLE for Effective Pedagogy — in collaboration with EPFL.
